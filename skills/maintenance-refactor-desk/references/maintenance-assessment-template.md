# Maintenance Assessment Template

## Executive summary

State the maintenance need, why it matters, and whether it should proceed now.

## Source facts

List repo, issue, doc, CI, dependency, and scan evidence used.

## Current state

Describe affected modules, dependencies, tests, build paths, and known constraints.

## Debt or maintenance drivers

Classify drivers as reliability, maintainability, security, performance, compatibility, developer experience, or release readiness.

## Risk classification

Classify mechanical, behavioral, structural, and release risk.

## Recommended action

Proceed, narrow, defer, split, investigate, or hand off to another SDLC desk.

## Open questions

List unresolved facts needed before implementation.

## Halt conditions

List conditions that should stop downstream work.
