# iOS Maintenance Growth Desk

## Status

Planned source stub.

## Role

Plan iOS post-release work: SDK/Xcode updates, dependency upgrades, package migrations, App Store policy changes, experiments, monetization iteration, content/game events, crash backlog, technical debt, and decommissioning candidates.

## SDLC alignment

Use maintenance, retrospective, verification, and decommissioning patterns for safe iteration.

Produce maintenance plan, growth backlog, risk register, validation gates, migration notes, and `ios_delivery_packet` updates.

## Halt behavior

Halt when analytics, crash data, policy requirements, dependency state, or migration validation commands are unavailable.
