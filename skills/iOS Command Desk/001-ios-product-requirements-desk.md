# iOS Product Requirements Desk

## Status

Planned source stub.

## Role

Turn iOS app/game intent into source-grounded requirements with target users, devices, OS range, app/game surface, monetization assumptions, App Store constraints, acceptance criteria, non-goals, risks, and open questions.

## SDLC alignment

Use `product-requirements-desk` patterns for requirement IDs and acceptance gates. Keep iOS-specific platform facts explicit before downstream handoff.

## Output contract

Produce requirements, acceptance criteria, risk register, open questions, and `ios_delivery_packet` updates.

## Halt behavior

Halt when product goal, target audience, app/game mode, supported devices, compliance/store constraints, or monetization assumptions are missing.
