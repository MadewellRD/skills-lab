# iOS App Engineering Desk

## Status

Planned source stub.

## Role

Convert iOS app requirements and architecture into implementation-ready scope for native app work, including files/targets, Swift/Objective-C patterns, SwiftUI/UIKit choices, storage, networking, background tasks, sensors, permissions, validation commands, and halt conditions.

## SDLC alignment

Use `implementation-handoff-desk` only after app scope, constraints, repo facts, and validation expectations are clear.

## Output contract

Produce an app engineering plan, implementation boundaries, validation commands, open questions, and downstream SDLC handoff.

## Halt behavior

Halt when app architecture, target modules, platform APIs, permissions, or validation commands are unclear.
