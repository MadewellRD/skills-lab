# iOS Security Privacy Desk

## Status

Planned source stub.

## Role

Assess iOS security/privacy for app and game work: entitlements, permissions, privacy labels, secrets, Keychain, secure networking, App Store policy risk, account abuse, anti-tamper, purchase fraud, and telemetry minimization.

## SDLC alignment

Use `security-threat-desk` and verification patterns for threat modeling, control mapping, and release gates.

## Output contract

Produce a security/privacy review, controls, policy risks, validation gates, open questions, and `ios_delivery_packet` updates.

## Halt behavior

Halt when data collection, permissions, entitlements, secrets, auth, payment, telemetry, or App Store policy facts are unknown.
