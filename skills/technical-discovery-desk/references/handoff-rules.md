# Downstream Handoff Rules

## To product-requirements-desk

Route back to product requirements when:

- User value is unclear
- Acceptance criteria are missing
- Scope or non-goals are disputed
- The technical path depends on a product decision

## To architecture-design-desk

Route to architecture design when:

- Feasibility is established
- Major options are identified
- Tradeoffs need an ADR, design spec, or interface contract

## To issue-planning-desk

Route to issue planning when:

- The work can be decomposed into trackable tasks
- Dependencies are known
- Acceptance gates can be assigned per issue

## To implementation-handoff-desk

Route to Implementation Handoff Desk only when:

- The repo and branch target are known
- Files or modules likely to change are known
- Validation commands are known or discoverable
- Risks are bounded enough for implementation
- No blocker unknowns remain
