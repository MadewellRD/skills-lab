# Severity Rubric

Use this rubric unless the organization has a stricter policy.

## SEV0 / Critical

Widespread production outage, data loss/corruption, active security/privacy exposure, or business-critical workflow unavailable with no workaround.

## SEV1 / High

Major customer-facing degradation, partial outage of critical capability, high-impact regression, or elevated operational risk with limited workaround.

## SEV2 / Medium

Degraded non-critical functionality, contained regression, limited customer impact, or workaround available.

## SEV3 / Low

Minor defect, documentation gap, cosmetic issue, or low-risk operational concern.

## Unknown

Use `severity unknown` when impact, scope, or exposure cannot be verified. List the specific facts needed to classify severity.

Severity must be evidence-backed. Do not downgrade because the fix seems simple. Do not upgrade solely because the report is urgent unless impact supports it.
