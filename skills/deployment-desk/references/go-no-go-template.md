# Go/No-Go Review Template

Use this for deployment readiness decisions.

## Decision

Choose one:

- Go
- Go with constraints
- No-go
- Hold pending evidence

## Required evidence

- Release scope confirmed:
- CI/checks green or exception approved:
- Verification evidence accepted:
- Security/privacy gates cleared:
- Rollback tested or documented:
- Observability ready:
- Owner coverage confirmed:
- Communication plan ready:

## Blockers

List each blocker with owner, source, severity, and required resolution.

## Constraints

List any rollout limits, monitoring windows, excluded environments, approval conditions, or risk acceptances.

## Decision record

Record who made the decision, when, based on which sources, and what must happen next.
