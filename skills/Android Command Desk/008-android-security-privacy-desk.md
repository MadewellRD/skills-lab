# Android Security Privacy Desk

## Status

Planned source stub.

## Role

Assess Android security/privacy for app and game work: permissions, data safety, secrets, secure storage, networking, certificate handling, Play policy risk, account abuse, anti-tamper, purchase fraud, and telemetry minimization.

## SDLC alignment

Use `security-threat-desk` and verification patterns for threat modeling, control mapping, and release gates.

## Output contract

Produce a security/privacy review, controls, policy risks, validation gates, open questions, and `android_delivery_packet` updates.

## Halt behavior

Halt when data collection, permissions, secrets, auth, payment, telemetry, or Play policy facts are unknown.
